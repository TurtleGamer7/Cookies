Something Gone Wrong

If something is not right about your cookies, maybe its one of the following

  • Dough to liquidy? Add a little more flour.
  • Not baking fast enough or burning? Adjust temperature as needed.
  • Rising too much? Add less baking soda flour.
  • Sinking? Add less baking powder or more flour.
