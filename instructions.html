Instructions

How to assemble

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix wet ingredients im a bowl until it combines and looks similar to oatmeal
  3. Add your dry ingredients on top of the wet ingredient combination
  4. Stir the dry ingredients together without combining the wet ingredients.
  5. Combine everything
  6. Add your chocolate chips and mix
  7. Line up your tray with parchment paper and spray it with oil
  8. Using your scooper, scoop 4-5 balls of dough and put it on the tray
  9. Bake at 300°F for 15 min or until cooked

    10. Now, your done!
